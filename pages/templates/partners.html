Summary

The RollSafe Platform is a digital platform that connects all of the players in the journey management value chain and ensures value is distributed transparently within that ecosystem. There are 3 major players in the RollSafe ecosystem namely the clients, partners and resources used to facilitate the journey end to end.

Partners are also either corporate bodies or individuals that collaborate with the platform to ensure availability, effectiveness and efficiency of the supply chain which completes the entire service delivery model.

I single handedly built the front-end of the partner-facing side of this website from start to finish with Angular, Typescript, Bootstrap, NGRX, HTML, SCSS and integrated all APIs .
