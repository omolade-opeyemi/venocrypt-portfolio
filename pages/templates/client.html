Summary

The RollSafe Platform is a digital platform that connects all of the players in the journey management value chain and ensures value is distributed transparently within that ecosystem. There are 3 major players in the RollSafe ecosystem namely the clients, partners and resources used to facilitate the journey end to end.

Clients are either corporate bodies or individuals that want to enjoy the ease and convenience of booking secured trips on familiar routes for either themselves, for others or and for cargo/merchandize.

I single handedly built the front-end of the client-facing side of this website from start to finish with Angular, Typescript, Bootstrap, NGRX, HTML, SCSS and integrated all APIs .
